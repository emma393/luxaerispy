Frankfurt is more than a transfer point: THE city balances skyline views, THE Main riverfront, serious museums, AND one of Europe’s strongest premium long-haul airports.

What to do

Frankfurt is best enjoyed with a mix of landmark time, neighborhood wandering, AND one or two standout meals rather than a checklist sprint across THE city.

  • Walk THE Römerberg AND NEW Old Town before heading toward THE museum embankment.
  • Use THE Main Tower AND riverfront FOR skyline views AND evening walks.
  • Leave time FOR Sachsenhausen if you want traditional taverns AND a more local pace.
  • Frankfurt also works well as a springboard to THE Rhine Valley if you have an extra DAY.

Where to stay

Choose your base by how you want THE trip to feel: closer to shopping AND museums, more residential AND quiet, or steps from nightlife AND evening dining.

Luxury stays: Sofitel Frankfurt Opera, JW Marriott Hotel Frankfurt, Roomers Frankfurt, Steigenberger Icon Frankfurter HOF.
