What to do in Frankfurt

Frankfurt combines a major long-haul hub, polished business infrastructure, riverfront districts, museums, and quick access to wine country and the Rhine Valley.

  • The Main Tower and skyline district for city views
  • Museumsufer and the Städel Museum for art and culture
  • The Römerberg and Old Town for historic architecture
  • Day trips to the Rhine Valley and Heidelberg
  • River Main walks and dining in Sachsenhausen

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Jumeirah Frankfurt, Sofitel Opera, and Villa Kennedy-style stays for polished service
  • Goethestraße shopping and discreet business dining suit premium travelers
  • Frankfurt is especially useful for travelers connecting deep into Europe

Dining and local flavor

  • Lafleur for Michelin-level dining
  • Restaurant Gustav for classic fine dining
  • Sachsenhausen taverns for local apple wine and regional dishes

Best suited for

  • business trips with efficient airport access
  • European gateway stopovers
  • city breaks paired with Rhine wine country
