New York (JFK, LGA, EWR)

New York blends iconic landmarks, celebrated restaurants, luxury hotels, Broadway, museums, and neighborhood culture into one of the world’s most rewarding city breaks.

What to do in New York

New York blends iconic landmarks, celebrated restaurants, luxury hotels, Broadway, museums, and neighborhood culture into one of the world’s most rewarding city breaks.

  • Central Park and The Met for classic Manhattan culture
  • The High Line, Chelsea Market, and Hudson Yards for modern city views
  • Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island for a classic first visit
  • Broadway and Lincoln Center for evening entertainment
  • Brooklyn Bridge, DUMBO, and Williamsburg for skyline views and dining

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Fifth Avenue shopping for Cartier, Bergdorf Goodman, Saks, and Tiffany & Co.
  • Aman New York, The Plaza, The St. Regis, and The Baccarat Hotel for top stays
  • Private helicopter rides, yacht charters, and VIP museum access for special occasions

Dining and local flavor

  • Le Bernardin for seafood
  • Per Se for tasting menus with park views
  • The Grill and Monkey Bar for power lunch or classic New York nights

Best suited for

  • long weekends with shopping and dining
  • premium leisure trips tied to shows or events
  • business travel extended with luxury experiences
