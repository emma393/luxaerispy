What to do in London

London combines heritage landmarks, elite hotels, world museums, theatre, and luxury shopping with easy access to multiple airports and efficient rail links.

  • Buckingham Palace, Westminster, and St James’s for iconic London
  • The British Museum, National Gallery, and V&A for culture
  • Covent Garden, Soho, and the West End for theatre and nightlife
  • Notting Hill and Kensington for elegant neighborhood walks
  • Day trips to Windsor, Oxford, or the Cotswolds

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Harrods, Bond Street, and Mount Street for designer shopping
  • Claridge’s, The Connaught, The Dorchester, and The Savoy for legendary stays
  • Private black-car touring, Thames dining cruises, and VIP theatre seating

Dining and local flavor

  • Core by Clare Smyth for fine dining
  • Gymkhana for a polished Mayfair dinner
  • The Ritz Restaurant for a classic London splurge

Best suited for

  • family city breaks with museums and parks
  • shopping-led luxury weekends
  • corporate trips with top hotel and dining access
