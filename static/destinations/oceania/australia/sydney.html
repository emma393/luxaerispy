Sydney

Sydney delivers one of THE easiest premium-city combinations in THE world: harbour views, iconic landmarks, beach time, AND long-haul routes that genuinely reward a lie-flat seat.

What to do

Sydney is best enjoyed with a mix of landmark time, neighborhood wandering, AND one or two standout meals rather than a checklist sprint across THE city.

  • Start with Circular Quay, THE Opera House, AND a harbour ferry to understand THE city layout.
  • Use THE Harbour Bridge area, Barangaroo, AND THE Rocks FOR waterside walks AND dining.
  • Keep a beach DAY FOR Bondi or Manly if THE weather is good.
  • ADD a DAY trip to THE Blue Mountains or THE Hunter Valley if you have more time.

Where to stay

Choose your base by how you want THE trip to feel: closer to shopping AND museums, more residential AND quiet, or steps from nightlife AND evening dining.

  • Circular Quay FOR landmark access.
  • Barangaroo FOR modern hotels AND dining.
  • Surry Hills AND Potts Point FOR a more local city base.

Luxury stays: Park Hyatt Sydney, Capella Sydney, Four Seasons Hotel Sydney, Crown Towers Sydney.
