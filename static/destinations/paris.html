What to do in Paris

Paris rewards travelers with landmark architecture, museums, couture shopping, café culture, and easy day trips, making it one of the most complete city destinations in Europe.

  • The Eiffel Tower, Trocadéro, and Seine river walks for classic city views
  • The Louvre, Musée d’Orsay, and Musée Rodin for world-class art
  • Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur for charm, views, and neighborhood dining
  • A Versailles day trip for palaces and gardens
  • Saint-Germain and Le Marais for boutiques, galleries, and cafés

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Avenue Montaigne and Place Vendôme for couture and jewelry
  • Le Meurice, Ritz Paris, Plaza Athénée, and Cheval Blanc Paris for luxury stays
  • Private museum access, river dinner cruises, and chauffeured shopping days

Dining and local flavor

  • Septime for modern French dining
  • Le Cinq for formal fine dining
  • L’Ambroisie for a classic Michelin splurge

Best suited for

  • honeymoons and anniversary trips
  • art and fashion focused city breaks
  • paired itineraries with Champagne or Versailles
