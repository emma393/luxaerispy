Rome (FCO, CIA)

What to do in Rome

Rome mixes ancient monuments, Vatican art, elegant hotels, terrace dining, and easy access to Italy’s signature food and culture in a city that rewards slow exploration.

  • The Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill for ancient history
  • The Vatican Museums and St Peter’s Basilica for art and architecture
  • Trevi Fountain, the Spanish Steps, and Piazza Navona for iconic walks
  • Trastevere and Campo de’ Fiori for evening dining and neighborhood life
  • Day trips to Tivoli or quick extensions to Florence and the Amalfi Coast

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Hotel de Russie, Hassler Roma, Hotel Eden, and Bulgari Hotel Roma for luxury stays
  • Via Condotti and the Spanish Steps area for fashion and jewelry
  • Private after-hours museum tours and chauffeured countryside escapes

Dining and local flavor

  • Roscioli for Roman classics
  • La Pergola for a fine dining splurge
  • Armando al Pantheon for traditional dishes near historic sights

Best suited for

  • culture-led luxury itineraries
  • honeymoons with art and food
  • combined city-and-coast Italian journeys
