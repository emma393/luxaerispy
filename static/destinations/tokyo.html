What to do in Tokyo

Tokyo is one of the world’s most rewarding city destinations for food, design, shopping, nightlife, and cultural precision, with excellent luxury hotel and dining depth.

  • Shibuya, Harajuku, and Omotesandō for style and energy
  • Asakusa and Sensō-ji for tradition and history
  • Ginza for luxury shopping and polished dining
  • TeamLab, Roppongi, and Tokyo Midtown for contemporary culture
  • Day trips to Hakone, Nikko, or Mount Fuji viewpoints

Where to stay and how to plan

  • Aman Tokyo, The Peninsula Tokyo, Four Seasons Otemachi, and Bulgari Hotel Tokyo for high-end stays
  • Private sushi counters, tea experiences, and chauffeured shopping in Ginza
  • Tokyo’s precision rail and premium hotel service suit high-touch itineraries

Dining and local flavor

  • Sushi Saito-style omakase experiences via concierge
  • Narisawa for modern Japanese fine dining
  • Tempura and wagyu counters in Ginza or Nihonbashi

Best suited for

  • food-focused luxury travel
  • city stays paired with ryokan escapes
  • spring blossom and autumn foliage trips
