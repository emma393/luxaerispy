Africa Airport overview

Africa Airport (AFR) serves Africa. For premium travel, the most important decisions are usually terminal familiarity, lounge eligibility, transfer timing, and how quickly you can reach your hotel or meetings after arrival.

What premium travelers should plan for

  • Check terminal and airline lounge access before the day of travel, especially on partner-carrier itineraries.
  • Africa usually works best when you have a car or transfer pre-booked, especially on early departures or late arrivals.
  • Use airport hotels or central stays based on the timing of your arrival, not only the nightly rate.

Getting into Africa
