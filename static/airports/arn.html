Stockholm-Arlanda Airport (ARN)

Stockholm-Arlanda Airport is an important gateway for Stockholm, Sweden, and it matters most to premium travelers who care about lounge access, check-in flow, and smooth arrivals.

Stockholm-Arlanda Airport overview

Stockholm-Arlanda Airport (ARN) serves Stockholm, Sweden. For premium travel, the most important decisions are usually terminal familiarity, lounge eligibility, transfer timing, and how quickly you can reach your hotel or meetings after arrival.

What premium travelers should plan for

  • Check terminal and airline lounge access before the day of travel, especially on partner-carrier itineraries.
  • Stockholm usually works best when you have a car or transfer pre-booked, especially on early departures or late arrivals.
  • Use airport hotels or central stays based on the timing of your arrival, not only the nightly rate.

Getting into Stockholm

  • Compare taxi, rail, and private transfer options before landing so you can move quickly after immigration or baggage claim.
  • For early departures, confirm how long security and premium check-in typically take at this airport and terminal.
