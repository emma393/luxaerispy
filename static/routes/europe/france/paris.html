FranceAirports: CDG, ORY

What to plan in Paris

  • Reserve the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and a Seine cruise early if you want a smoother first visit.
  • Walk between the Tuileries, Place Vendôme, and Avenue Montaigne for shopping and classic Paris scenery.
  • Use Saint-Germain-des-Prés and Le Marais for cafés, galleries, and a slower street-level feel.
