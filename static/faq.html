Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about using the website and requesting premium flight options.

Do I need to choose a valid airport?

Yes. The search and request tools only work properly when you select real airports from the website’s airport list.

Do I need to enter real contact details?

Yes. Every request requires a valid email address or phone or WhatsApp number so the right specialist can reach you if follow-up is appropriate.

Does LuxAeris sell tickets directly?

No. LuxAeris is a premium travel research and lead platform. It helps travelers explore options and, when relevant, connects them with selected travel specialists or partner agencies.

Can I use the form for premium domestic US routes?

Yes. The request flow supports premium domestic US travel as well as long-haul international travel to and from the US.

Are the price ranges final quotes?

No. Pricing ranges are directional and are shown for planning context only.
