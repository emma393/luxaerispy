Privacy Policy

This page explains how website information, quote-request details, and basic usage data may be handled.

What information we collect

When you use the request form, we may collect your name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, preferred route, travel dates, cabin preferences, and notes you choose to share.

How we use information

We use request details to understand premium travel demand, improve route research across the website, and connect travelers with selected travel specialists or partner agencies when a match is relevant.

Partner sharing

If you ask for tailored options, your request may be shared with carefully selected travel partners so they can follow up with relevant premium travel assistance. We do not position the website as a direct ticket seller.

Analytics and cookies

The website may use cookies, analytics, and hosting logs to understand site usage, improve performance, and support a smoother browsing experience.

Data handling

Information is handled with reasonable safeguards appropriate for a lead-generation and travel research platform. You may contact us to request updates or removal of your submitted information where legally applicable.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect business, legal, or operational changes.
